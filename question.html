Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our eco‑friendly bags, custom orders, and sustainable practices.

ASK

Products & Materials

What materials are your eco bags made from?
+
We use a variety of sustainable materials: non woven polypropylene (recyclable), natural cotton canvas, jute (biodegradable), juco (jute‑cotton blend), and calico cotton. All materials are chosen for durability and minimal environmental impact.
Are your bags really eco‑friendly?
+
Yes! Our bags are designed to replace single use plastics. They are reusable, made from renewable or recycled materials, and many are biodegradable at end of life. We prioritize ethical production and low‑carbon manufacturing.
How do I care for my eco bag?
+
Most bags can be spot cleaned or gently hand washed with mild soap. Avoid machine washing canvas and jute to preserve shape. Air dry only. Each order includes care instructions.
What is the difference between non‑woven and canvas?
+
Non‑woven bags are lightweight, water resistant, and cost‑effective – great for events and retail. Canvas bags are heavier, made from cotton, and ideal for premium branding and daily use.

Orders & Customization

Can I order custom bags with my logo?
+
Absolutely! We specialize in custom branding. You can provide your artwork, and we'll offer screen printing, digital transfer, or embroidery. Minimum order quantity starts at 100 pieces.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
+
For custom printed bags, MOQ is usually 100 units. For plain stock bags, smaller quantities may be available. Contact us for specific requirements.
How long does production take?
+
Production typically takes 10–15 business days after design approval, plus shipping time. Bulk orders may require additional lead time. We'll provide a clear timeline upon inquiry.
Do you offer samples?
+
Yes, we provide digital mockups free of charge. Physical samples can be arranged for a fee (refundable with bulk order). This ensures you're happy with material and print quality.

Shipping & Returns

Do you ship worldwide?
+
Yes, we ship globally from Sri Lanka. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. We use reliable couriers and offer carbon‑offset options.
What is your return policy?
+
Because many items are custom‑made, returns are only accepted for manufacturing defects or errors on our part. Please inspect your order upon arrival and contact us within 7 days.
How much does shipping cost?
+
Shipping is calculated based on weight, volume, and destination. We'll provide a quote during the ordering process. Bulk orders often qualify for discounted freight.

Sustainability

How do you ensure ethical production?
+
We partner with certified workshops that provide fair wages, safe working conditions, and no child labor. We visit facilities regularly and maintain transparent supply chains.
Are your packaging materials eco‑friendly?
+
Yes, we use recycled cardboard, paper tape, and biodegradable poly bags when necessary. We minimize plastic in all our shipments.
Can I recycle my non‑woven bag?
+
Non‑woven polypropylene (PP) is recyclable in many facilities (check local guidelines). Better yet, reuse it as many times as possible before recycling.
