We are Eco Creational

Born from a simple idea: that everyday choices can reshape the future of our planet — one bag at a time.

From small steps to big impact

Eco Creational began in Sri Lanka with a handful of sewing machines and a deep concern for the plastic crisis. What started as a local initiative to replace single‑use bags has grown into a trusted partner for universities, resorts, and global brands.

We believe sustainability should never be a compromise. That’s why we blend timeless design with eco‑friendly materials — non‑woven, canvas, jute, and calico cotton — all crafted ethically. Every bag we produce carries a promise: to reduce waste, support conscious living, and empower communities.

Our core values

These principles guide every stitch, every design, and every partnership.

🌍

Planet first

Every material is chosen for its low environmental footprint. Biodegradable, recyclable, or reusable.

🤝

Ethical craft

Fair wages, safe working conditions, and a commitment to the artisans behind every bag.

✨

Design without waste

Minimalist, functional, and timeless — so you’ll want to reuse it again and again.

💡

Innovation for good

Continuously exploring better materials and processes to push sustainability forward.
